# Benchmark Datasets

The 3 synthetic L2 order-book datasets used to benchmark this solver vs the QUBO sibling.

- `small_3assets_4venues_20orders.json` — 3 assets, 4 venues, 20 orders (32 KB)
- `medium_8assets_8venues_100orders.json` — 8 assets, 8 venues, 100 orders (160 KB)
- `large_15assets_12venues_500orders.json` — 15 assets, 12 venues, 500 orders (453 KB)

Generated 2026-05-08 with realistic spreads, fees, and quality weights.
